  • Safe and accessible delivery and pickup conditions.

Standard cleaning is included in the rental price. Additional cleaning fees may apply for excessive mess, trash, bodily waste outside fixtures, smoking residue, or misuse.

The following items are strictly prohibited from being flushed or placed in the restroom system:
